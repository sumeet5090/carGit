About Us


SB Car Insurance

SB Car Insurance is a private sector general insurance company in India.

Founded in November 2016, the company received its license from the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) in September 2017.

follows an online-led model and hence all operations for the company are offered through the digital platform.


History

This general insurance company was founded by Sumeet Boga, and he serves as the Chief Executive Officer

As of 2018, SB has raised $42 million in funding from investors such as Amazon, Accel Partners, SAIF Partners, Catamaran Ventures.


Parnerships

SB Car Insurance partnered with Ola cabs and launched an in-trip insurance program in more than 110 cities in India

SB Car Insurance partnered with Amazon India to provide mobile insurance plans on the retailer's website
